Problem Statement

College students struggle to find time in the day to handle responsibilities and lose time to sleep because of it. Team: JJ, Ronald, Ezra, Philip.

Personas

Our group worked together to brainstorm 4 types of people that would benefit from the scheduling app our team is creating: Peter, John, Grace, and Alex.

Storyboards

Our group worked together to brainstorm ways in which our personas could apply our scheduling app, Sleep More, to their daily lives.

Sketches

Our group drew a series of app designs to determine the best and most user-friendly interface for Sleep More, including calendar views, settings, and schedule management screens.
